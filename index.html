Privacy Policy for ePubHub

Last Updated: October 26, 2024

Welcome to ePubHub ("we", "our", or "us"). We are committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, process, and distribute your information, including Personal Data, when you use our mobile application and related services.

1. Information We Collect

1.1. Information You Provide to Us

  • Account Information: When you register or sign in via Google Services, we collect your name, email address, profile picture URL, and a unique identifier (Firebase UID/User ID).
  • User Profile: Any information you choose to update in your profile, such as a custom username or updated avatar (processed via UCrop).
  • User-Generated Content (UGC): This includes messages, text, and media you send via our Chat feature, which are stored in the Firebase Realtime Database.
  • Shared Files: EPUB/TXT files you voluntarily upload to our "Cloud Share" feature (hosted via Cloudinary) to share with the community.

1.2. Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Identifiers: We collect your Android ID and device hardware model to manage sessions and enforce the limit of 2 active devices per account.
  • Usage Statistics: Reading progress (current chapter/line), total listening time, daily reading streaks, and game achievements (Gomoku/Chess) are collected to synchronize across your devices.
  • Log Data: In the event of an app crash, we may collect technical logs via Firebase Crashlytics to improve stability.

1.3. Sensitive Data Handling

PGP/GPG Data: Our application supports PGP/GPG decryption. All cryptographic operations occur locally on your device. We never upload or store your private keys or the decrypted content of your secure files on our servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To Provide Services: To manage your account, verify your subscription status (VIP/Admin), and synchronize your reading library.
  • To Enable Social Features: To facilitate chat interactions, P2P file transfers (using WebRTC signaling), and public file sharing.
  • To Improve the App: We use synthesized usage data to optimize our TTS (Text-to-Speech) performance and game balancing.
  • Security: To detect and prevent fraud, unauthorized device access, and to enforce our community guidelines.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. We share information only with the following service providers necessary for app functionality:

  • Google Firebase: For cloud hosting, authentication, and real-time database services.
  • Cloudinary: For hosting files shared by users via the "Cloud" feature.
  • Microsoft: When using Online TTS (Microsoft Edge TTS), text segments are sent to Microsoft's servers for audio synthesis. This data is processed temporarily and not stored.
  • Other Users: Your display name and profile picture are visible to other users when you participate in Chat or share files.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your information as long as your account is active. We provide a clear path for data removal:

  • In-App Deletion: You can use the "Delete Account" feature in the settings. This action triggers an automated process to delete your Firestore profile, Realtime Database messages, and Firebase Auth records.
  • Manual Request: You may contact us via email to request manual deletion of any data associated with your identity.

5. Children's Privacy

ePubHub does not knowingly collect or solicit personal information from anyone under the age of 13. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under age 13, we will delete that information as quickly as possible.

6. User Rights (GDPR/CCPA)

Depending on your location, you have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. You may also object to the processing of your data in certain circumstances.
