Privacy Policy (English)

Last updated: October 26, 2023

ePubHub ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: When you sign in via Google, we collect your Name, Email, and Profile Picture to manage your account.
  • User Content: If you use the Cloud feature, we upload your EPUB/TXT files to Cloudinary for sharing purposes. Otherwise, files are processed locally on your device.
  • Usage Data: We store reading progress, personal dictionaries, and game scores to enhance your experience.

2. Third-Party Services

We use the following services to provide app functionality:

  • Google Firebase: For authentication and secure data storage.
  • Cloudinary: For hosting shared files.
  • Microsoft Edge TTS: To provide Text-to-Speech services (data is processed temporarily).

3. Data Deletion & Retention

You have full control over your data. You can delete your account and all associated data directly within the app (Account tab). Once deleted, all personal info and cloud files are permanently removed from our servers.

4. Children's Privacy

Our services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.
